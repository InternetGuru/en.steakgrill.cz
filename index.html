Restaurant Concept STEAKGRILL® is a Czech concept developed by M. D. Klíma based on his twenty years of experience and work in American steakhouses. It differs from other restaurants in that it offers a wide range of popular international dishes in addition to steaks.

Our Steaks We make beef steaks from aged meat with a high degree of marbling. We roast our steaks accurately as you request—from rare to well done. If you are not sure our staff is always ready to advise you.
Rare
Medium rare
Medium

Our meats are never frozen. We make sure the meat reaches room temperature before cooking. We tenderize all meat by gentle manual means (no chemicals).

USDA Prime
USDA Choice
USDA Select

We import mostly from farms in South America, usually from Uruguay, with high and medium degrees of marbling. We use meat from grain-fed animals or USDA Prime and USDA Choice quality class meats.
